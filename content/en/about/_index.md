Welcome to the world of Sander Veen, a contemporary artist from Veenendaal, the Netherlands, who specialises in abstract art and surrealism. Sander is known for his unique style, combining highly evocative images with geometric shapes and vibrant colours. In his paintings the line between reality and imagination dissolves, which creates an enchanting and inspiring experience.

In his surrealist works, Sander combines elements from nature, historical themes and fantasy worlds in a special way. Each painting tells a story that is both recognisable and unreal. Through the use of symbolism and mysticism, he manages to create a mysterious atmosphere that invites the viewer to explore further.

Sander's abstract art goes beyond the visual. Inspired by the unconscious and spiritual emotions, he invites viewers to look deeper and discover new layers of meaning. His use of light, texture and powerful colours creates a unique connection between the inner feeling and the bigger picture.

He is self-taught and has found his own way in the art world. With several exhibitions to his name he knows how to engage his viewers in his personal quest for emotion, mysticism and connection.
