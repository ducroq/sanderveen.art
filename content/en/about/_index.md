Sander Veen is a Dutch artist working with oil paint, acrylic and mixed media. His work is characterized by the use of gold leaf, texture and expressive color palettes.

The paintings range from abstract to magical realism, exploring themes such as the human condition, spirituality and the power of nature.

Sander works from his studio and is available for studio visits by appointment. Feel free to contact him for more information or a personal viewing.
