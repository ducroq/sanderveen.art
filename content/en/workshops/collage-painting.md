Create a painting inspired by the story of a fellow participant. A workshop in 6 lessons that makes you look at the world around you differently.

In this special workshop series you listen to the stories of your fellow participants and translate their experiences into a visual artwork. It is an exercise in empathy, imagination and creative interpretation that produces surprising results.

**Practical information:**
- 6 lessons of 2 hours
- € 40 per lesson (materials included)
- Saturdays 10:00–12:00 or Wednesdays 14:00–16:00
- Location: Eenvoudlaan 6A, 3901 SP Veenendaal
