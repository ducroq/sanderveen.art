Let yourself be inspired by rhythms and sounds in 6 lessons, and translate the feeling of music onto your canvas.

During this workshop you learn how music can influence your painting process. You listen to different music styles and translate the emotions, rhythms and melodies into colours and shapes on the canvas. A unique experience where two art forms come together.

**Practical information:**
- 6 lessons of 2 hours
- € 40 per lesson (materials included)
- Saturdays 10:00–12:00 or Wednesdays 14:00–16:00
- Location: Eenvoudlaan 6A, 3901 SP Veenendaal
