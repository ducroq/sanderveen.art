From figurative to abstract in 6 lessons. Learn the art of leaving out and discover new ways of expression.

In this workshop series you make the transition from figurative to abstract painting. Step by step you learn how to simplify forms, experiment with colour and composition, and develop your own abstract visual language.

**Practical information:**
- 6 lessons of 2 hours
- € 40 per lesson (materials included)
