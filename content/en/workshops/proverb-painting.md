In 6 lessons you create a unique painting inspired by a self-invented proverb. Let your creativity run free!

Invent your own proverb and translate it into a painting. In this workshop series you learn how to turn a concept into a visual story. From idea to execution you work on a personal artwork that brings your proverb to life.

**Practical information:**
- 6 lessons of 2 hours
- € 40 per lesson (materials included)
- Saturdays 10:00–12:00 or Wednesdays 14:00–16:00
- Location: Eenvoudlaan 6A, 3901 SP Veenendaal
