In 6 lessons you paint away your stress and create a painting that radiates happiness.

This workshop combines painting with relaxation. You learn how to release tension through the creative process and capture positive energy on the canvas. The result is not only a beautiful painting, but also a feeling of calm and fulfilment.

**Practical information:**
- 6 lessons of 2 hours
- € 40 per lesson (materials included)
- Saturdays 10:00–12:00 or Wednesdays 14:00–16:00
